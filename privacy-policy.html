Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 18 February 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BlinkBattle ("we," "us," or "our"), a simple mobile game. BlinkBattle does not require an account and does not send your data to any servers we control. Your game data and settings are stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information. Specifically:

  • No account or identity data (no email, name, or password).
  • No analytics, cookies, pixels, or tracking technologies.
  • No server-side databases or cloud sync by default.

2.1 Data Stored on Your Device

  • Game Data: scores, game history, and progress—saved only via local storage on your device.
  • Settings: preferences like sound or theme—also saved locally.

3. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used solely within the app to display and manage your game progress on your device. We do not process your data on remote servers.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to third parties. Because data is stored locally, we do not have access to it.

5. Storage, Security & Deletion

  • Storage: Data is saved on-device storage in the mobile app.
  • Security: Local storage is controlled by your device. Keep your device secure to protect your data.
  • Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data, or use the app's reset feature if available.

6. Children's Privacy

BlinkBattle is a general-purpose game and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy if the app's functionality changes. We will update the effective date above and publish the new version here.
